## Detailed description

Hi Energy AI brings affiliate marketing intelligence into Google Workspace.

**Search without leaving Gmail, Sheets, Docs, Slides, Drive, or Calendar**
Run universal search across advertisers, deals, transactions, and contacts. Results come from the Hi Energy AI MCP server with the same permissions as your Hi Energy account.

**Export up to 500 rows to Google Sheets**
Pick a type — Advertisers, Deals, Transactions, Contacts, or Everything — enter a query, and the add-on paginates the API until your sheet is complete. Transactions can be filtered by advertiser and 30/60/90-day window. When used inside Sheets, results are added as tabs in the active spreadsheet.

**Download any report you have access to**
A dedicated Reports surface filters the MCP tool list to anything report-shaped and runs each one straight into a Google Sheet. Every other MCP tool result is one tap away from export, too.

**One-tap draft email to any contact**
Every contact row — in search results, contact cards, and lookup — has a Draft button that opens a prefilled Gmail draft enriched with advertiser and deal context.

**Gmail context on every email**
When you open a message, the add-on shows the sender domain, matching Google Contacts, recent Gmail threads from that domain, and one-click lookup in Hi Energy AI.

**Two ways to connect**
Sign in with Hi Energy AI (Auth0) or paste a per-user API key. Backend URL overrides are available in Settings → Advanced. Your Google Gmail and Contacts data stays in Google; only you can access your mailbox and contacts. Hi Energy data is fetched over HTTPS with your bearer token or API key.

**Who it's for**
Affiliate managers, partnership teams, and Hi Energy AI customers who live in Gmail and need fast access to program data while reading partner email.
